## Description

ArcFlow Finance is the native DeFi hub for Arc Network, offering lending, borrowing, liquidity provision, and yield optimization services. It leverages Arc's USDC-native gas to provide predictable transaction costs for DeFi operations.

## Allowed Actions

- `deposit` - Deposit assets into lending pools
- `withdraw` - Withdraw assets from pools
- `stake` - Stake tokens for rewards
- `unstake` - Unstake tokens
- `claim_rewards` - Claim earned rewards
- `borrow` - Borrow against collateral
- `repay` - Repay borrowed assets

## Key Features

1. **Stablecoin-Optimized Lending** - Designed for USDC and stablecoin assets
2. **Predictable Fees** - USDC gas means consistent transaction costs
3. **Fast Liquidations** - Sub-second finality enables efficient liquidations
4. **Isolated Pools** - Risk-isolated lending markets
