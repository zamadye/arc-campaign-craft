## Tone Guidelines

Social campaigns should feel:
- **Authentic** - Real human voice, not corporate
- **Inclusive** - Welcome newcomers
- **Curious** - Explore, don't preach
- **Humble** - Share learnings, not guarantees

## Required Elements

1. Human perspective or story
2. Genuine observation about Arc
3. No financial promises
4. Welcoming tone

## Caption Structure

```
[Personal observation/story] + [What I noticed about Arc] + [Genuine reaction] + #ArcNetwork
```

## Community Guidelines

- Celebrate milestones without overpromising
- Share genuine experiences
- Welcome questions and curiosity
- Never gatekeep or create exclusivity
